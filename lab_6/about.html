About 3D Printing Central

We are a small company with very few resources at hand. While we may not have the best 3D printing technology at our fingertips, we are able to answer most questions and concerns for hobbyists.

Overall, this website is used as a resource to post info for hobbyists and provide an easy to access portal for communicating with our team. If you need any assistance at all, please navigate to the contact page and feel free to reach out.
