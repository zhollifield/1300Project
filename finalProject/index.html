Benefits of Multi-Color 3D Printing

  1. Increased detail due to contrast of colors.
  2. Endless possibilites with a little creativity.

Cons of Multi-Color 3D Printing

  1. Increased waste due to purging of colored filament.
  2. Increased print time due to purge time.
