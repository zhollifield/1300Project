About Us

I am a the sole spirit keeping the site alive. This is mainly a passion project trying to bring more hobbyists into the realm of multi-color printing. While I am not a master by any means, I have learned a lot over the course of my research and believe it is worth sharing. The possibilities are truly endless and I'd like to see more users tinkering with multi-color prints so that we can develop the processes further.

With time comes innvocation, so if you have any updates or concerns that you'd like to share with me, feel free to reach out via the Contact page> I am always open to feedback and would love to see what can come of a very niche hobby.

Hardware I Am Currently Running

  • AnyCubic Predator with BMG extruder
  • Ender 3 with many upgrades.
  • 3018 CNC Machine for custom part fabrication.
